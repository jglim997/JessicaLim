"Why human-centered design?"

I am passionate about human-centered design, because it embodies so much of what I value and look to achieve in my career. Human-centered design focuses solely on the user and in making their experiences seamless and positive. I truly believe in importance of good UX design for any product that customers come across and in the ways that it can accomodate for all the ways that a users wishes to interact with a given product or interface.

Human-centered design requires empathy and thought over thought for the user, for others.
